Privacy Policy for HuzBrowser

Effective Date: January 12, 2025

HuzBrowser ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our application ("HuzBrowser"). By using the app, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

HuzBrowser is designed with your privacy in mind. We operate in permanent private browsing mode, which means:

  • No History or Cookies Saved: Your browsing history, cookies, and sessions are erased when you exit the browser.
  • No Personal Information Collected: We do not collect or store any personally identifiable information (PII), such as your name, email address, or location.
  • No Tracking: We do not use tracking tools or analytics to monitor your activity within the app.

2. Permissions We Use

HuzBrowser requires certain permissions to function optimally. These permissions and their purposes are listed below:

Required Permissions

  • INTERNET: To access web pages and provide browsing functionality.
  • ACCESS_WIFI_STATE: To detect and utilize Wi-Fi networks.
  • ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: To check the network status for internet connectivity.
  • WAKE_LOCK: To keep the app active during downloads or when viewing media.

Optional Permissions

  • WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE: To allow file downloads and access to media files on your device (only for Android versions up to SDK 32).
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS: To notify users about downloads or other critical app updates.
  • SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW: To allow floating windows or overlays during browsing.
  • SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM: For scheduling and managing downloads.

HuzBrowser uses these permissions solely to provide its features. We do not use them to collect data or track your activity.

3. Ads and Third-Party Services

HuzBrowser integrates with Google AdMob to display ads within the app. While we do not collect any personal data, Google AdMob may collect data as per their privacy policies. For more information, refer to the Google AdMob Privacy Policy.

4. Data Security

Although HuzBrowser does not collect personal data, we take measures to secure your browsing activity:

  • Safe Browsing: We enable Android's WebView Safe Browsing feature to protect against malicious websites.
  • Temporary Data Storage: All data is stored temporarily during your browsing session and is permanently deleted upon exiting the app.
